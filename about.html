About

Mission Statement

The irok Foundation is a non-profit foundation that helps families with children diagnosed with cancer or other blood disorders. We’ve helped 172 Indiana families!!

Additional Information

The irok Foundation was founded in 2009 by Kori Brown. Kori has a blood disorder that she has dealt with her entire life. In 2009, Extreme Makeover made her and her family a new house and CVS granted her $50,000 to start up the foundation. Kori has been at the forefront of the foundation since the beginning and has played a part in helping all 172 families.
